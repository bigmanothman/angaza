Meet the Founder

The visionary behind ANGAZA — transforming employment connections across Africa through innovation, dedication, and technical excellence.

Azania High School

Othman Almas Mwiha

Founder & CEO

A passionate technologist and innovator from Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Currently a senior student at Azania High School, Othman is on a mission to revolutionize how Africa connects talent with opportunity.

His vision for ANGAZA was born from a simple observation: talented individuals struggle to find meaningful work, while employers struggle to find qualified candidates. The solution? A platform that bridges this gap with intelligence, transparency, and African ingenuity.
